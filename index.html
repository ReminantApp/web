EMERGENCY BUTTON

Fast help in case of emergency

With a tap on the emergency button, you can quickly inform selected contacts in an emergency. Your current location will be transmitted immediately. You can alert your contacts by email - with KommGutHeim Plus even by call & SMS!

To trigger emergencies even more intuitively and discreetly, you can optionally pair a KommGutHeim Bluetooth button. Your smartphone can stay in your pocket, just hold the button for 2 seconds.
